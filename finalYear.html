About Project

During my time at final year at A.I.T I worked on a golfing app for my local golf club. I choose this project as it combined two things I enjoy doing programming with the android SDK and golf. The app would give you all the information you needed to have a great day at Claremorris Golf Club. Features included Weather reports based on the user prefreed weather condtions for golfing, Yardage Book, Bar Menu with abillity to ring in your order, Scorecard and directions to the course.

The app would have also kept a live update of competion scores and and friends best scores of week month and all time best score for you to compete against friends. However due to the a PC failure and my personal navivty of not using git I had to restart all work again and complete with thesis in 7 weeks. Leaving the final polish the app needed missing and yet to be completed.
