About Project

During my time at Transact Carbon. A personal highlight though was taking the carbon calculator app from concept right through to a working prototype. The app interacted with the Transact Carbon carbon credit api. It calculated the ammount of carbon credits that you need to offset your carbon footprint, then allowed you to choose a carbon project to support by purchasing your Carbon Credits from that project.

The app would have also included gamification elements that would allow you to see where your ranked among your friends to see who was the most eco conscious. Unfortunately though my time at as an intern ended before we could launch the app. I also worked on a carbon footprint awareness game and tested their carbon credit api during my time their where we were able to achieve 99% test coverage.
